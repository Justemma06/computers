• Trackball

    A trackball is a pointing device consisting of a ball held by a socket containing sensors to detect a rotation of the ball about two axes-like an upside-down ball mouse with an exposed protruding ball.

  • Touchpad

    A touchpad is a pointing device featuring a tactile sensor,a specialized surface that can translate the motion and position of a user's fingers to a relative position on the operating system that is made output to screen.

  • Joystick

    A joystick, sometimes called a flight stick, is an input device consisting of a stick that pivots on a base and reports its angle or direction to the device.
